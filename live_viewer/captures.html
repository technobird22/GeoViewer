About this site

This viewer displays near real time images from the satellite, GK-2A. You can select various enhancements to view, and you can hover over the image for a higher resolution, magnified view.

About the data

This data was received from the South Korean satellite Geokompsat-2A, abbreviated "GK-2A". These will be in near real-time, and consist of data from imagers on the satellite as well as a variety of other sources, including, but not limited to, rebroadcasts of the Korean Weather Agency, Future weather predictions, Downlink schedules, and more.
You can see Japan, Australia, New Zealand, as well as a variety of other countries on the full disc frames.
